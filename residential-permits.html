Residential drafting support

Permit-oriented drawings that keep the project moving

Clear, consistent drawing sets reduce back-and-forth, prevent surprises, and help approvals move faster. The goal is simple: a package that reads cleanly and matches reality.

Services

  • Drafting support for permit submissions (project-dependent)
  • Existing-condition drawings (from site measures + client info)
  • Plan / elevation / section drafting as required
  • Detail callouts and typical construction notes (as needed)
  • Coordination support with builders and stakeholders

Tools

  • AutoCAD (2D drafting and permit set workflow)
  • Fusion 360 (3D context + details when beneficial)
  • PDF deliverables with organized sheet sets and revisions

Remote-friendly Local site visits (when required)

Typical Lead Times

  • Small drafting updates: 3–7 business days
  • Typical residential package: 2–4 weeks
  • Rush options: case-by-case

Lead time depends on scope and how quickly measurements, photos, and decisions are provided.

Budget Ranges

  • Small revisions / redraws: $250–$900
  • Small permit drafting support: $900–$2,500
  • Larger / more complex scope: $2,500–$6,500+

Final pricing depends on scope, completeness of inputs, and revision requirements.

Project Examples

  • Deck / porch drawings
  • Interior renovation layout updates
  • Accessory structures (shed / outbuilding)
  • As-built redraws for planning and quoting
  • Coordination drawings for contractors and trades

Good Inputs = Fast Output

  • Clear measurements (or site visit details)
  • Photos from multiple angles
  • Scope + timeline
  • Any municipal requirements already received
